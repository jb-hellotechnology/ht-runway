Most sites take three to six weeks, depending on size and how quickly you can get me your words and photos. I'll give you a realistic timeline up front.

Yes — I build sites that are easy to edit and show you how. Or if you'd rather not, just email me and I'll sort it.

Every bit. No outsourcing, no account managers — you deal with me from first chat to launch and beyond.
